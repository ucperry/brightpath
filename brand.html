The Three Pillars (Lineage-Driven)

These pillars are not just categories—they form a living chain of evidence that connects big-picture governance to working code. That chain makes scope evolution safe, fast, and accountable after MVP.

Methodology (Governance Principles)

Defines how value is judged, risk is managed, and decisions are made. Establishes the rules that every downstream design and work item must satisfy.

Blueprint (Architecture & Standards)

Translates principles into concrete architecture, patterns, and technical guardrails. Every component and integration traces back to a blueprint decision.

Playbook (Execution & Traceability)

Documents plan-vs-actual and links Jira epics/stories/tasks to Blueprint decisions and Methodology principles—maintaining a complete chain of evidence.

Bi-Directional Lineage

Top-down: Principles ➜ Architecture ➜ Work Items (requirements enforced at every step)
Bottom-up: Any task ➜ linked to its Blueprint decision ➜ linked to its governing Principle

“After MVP, we adapt quickly because every change is traceable to its rationale—no guesswork, no rework.”

Safe Scope Evolution

Impact of any change is known in seconds. Decisions stay aligned with principles and architecture.

Faster Iterations

Clear lineage removes coordination drag; teams ship improvements rapidly post-MVP.

Accountable Decisions

Every work item carries its rationale. Reviews and audits become evidence-based, not opinion-based.
